# Privacy Policy — Tablomino

_Last updated: 2026-07-16_

Tablomino collects **no personal data, of any kind, from anyone, ever.**

- Everything the app stores — player profiles, practice progress, badges,
  settings — is saved only on your own device, in your browser's local
  IndexedDB storage. None of it is transmitted anywhere.
- There is no account, no sign-in, and no server. The app has no backend to
  send data to.
- There are no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising, and no
  third-party SDKs of any kind embedded in the app.
- The only network requests the app ever makes are to load the app itself
  (and, once installed, none at all — it works fully offline).

This applies equally to children using the app: Tablomino does not collect,
store, or share any personal information about any user, child or adult,
under any circumstance.

Uninstalling the app or clearing your browser's site data removes all stored
information immediately and completely, since nothing exists anywhere else.
